Clic’n Clac is here to make finding interns much easier. Our goal is to save you time by taking out third party recruitment agencies and provide you with the tools needed for you to find the intern you need. We do all this and far more, and it’s 100% free.

Our Platform

You can upload as many positions as needed and set the criteria needed for the position, this way the candidates that apply already correspond to the basic criteria set by you. We envision many candidates applying towards your positions so we also provide an organizer for you to easily keep track of all the candidates that apply to each position and where they are at in your recruitment process. Yes, it's still Free.

Your Candidates

Each candidate is limited to the number of internships (5 or 10) that they can apply towards simultaneously, and are given your company details before they apply. This method ensures that candidates have considered your role and company before applying, giving you more serious candidates.

Once candidates apply you can download their resumes, arrange interviews, and chat directly with them. We’ll also show you things you won’t find on their resumes, like key strengths, availabilities, and more.

What Makes Us Standout?

Clic'n Clac was created to put an end to ridiculous prices candidates have to pay to find your internship offer. No more back and forth emails between you and a third party just for the one candidate they propose.  Finished are the days of hundreds of candidates that don't remotley correspond to your position like you get from a typical job board. Clic’n Clac is here to upgrade the internship market, and with us, you will find the right fit for your position and you’ll save time doing so.

Clic’n Clac is 100% Free: Create your account and post your internship position(s) while selecting the criteria needed for a candidate to be able to apply to your position.

Specific Network: Benefit from our network that is changing how companies find candidates by bringing all different nationalities and profiles directly to you.

Digital Recruitment Platform: Benefit from our powerful recruitment platform where you can study profiles, chat with candidates, arrange interviews, and find the right candidate. All on one platform, all completely free.

Our Relationship

Our only requirement is that you are actively searching for a candidate, this is why our platform is designed to keep candidates informed of your interest or non-interest of their profiles. We are not a job board where candidates can apply to hundreds of positions only to become discouraged and unmotivated from the lack of responses they receive. Our platform is designed for candidates to study and consider each position before they commit to applying, so we encourage timely responses.
