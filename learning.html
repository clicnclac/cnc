Learning Center

We don't just provide you with an awesome network of companies, we also want to give you the tools to find that internship you are looking for.  So this is why we provide a FREE learning center where we cover the topics
needed to assist you during the recruitment process.

Tips and Advice 

Clic'n Clac is here by your side. You can find everything you need in the Learning Center to become the perfect candidate. Every topic, we offer some tips and advice for every step during the recruitment process. We do this for Free, so you can start creating that everlasting experience you are looking for.

Use our Models

Not only do we provide expert advice, we have provided some templates for your Cover Letters and CV's so you have somewhere to start.  You can pick the one that suits you the best, and start editing it to fit your style!


Talk to our Experts

We have over 20 years of combined experience in the International Internship field. We know what companies are looking for, and what candidates need to do. We are here to help, get in touch! For a small fee, we can coach you throughout the recruitment process.
